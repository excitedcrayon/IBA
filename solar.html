Solar

Izuba Solar Solutions exists to promote, develop and deliver turnkey Solar PV projects to ensure the availability of sustainable and renewable energy in Africa.

We want to see all corners of Africa with adequate and sustainable power to ensure the social and economic advancement of the African people.

Grid Tied Solar PV Systems

  • PV System connected to the grid
  • System seldomly has batteries, but uses the grid for storage

Grid Tied Systems With Battery Back-up

  • Batteries power selected loads when the utility grid is down
  • Inverter does not feed into the grid when the grid is down

Off-Grid Systems

  • System is independent of the electricity grid
  • PV Modules harness solar energy to power loads and change batteries
  • Batteries used to store energy for periods when solar modules are not producing the required amounts of energy

Direct PV Systems

  • PV Module used to power system directly
  • Solar pump, fan or cellphone charger
  • Often used in remote areas for water pumping
