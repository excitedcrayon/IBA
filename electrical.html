Multi Point Guard Monitoring System (GMS)

This wireless system is designed for real-time monitoring of guards on patrol. It caters for a maximum of 20 guards on site and up to 20 supervisors visiting the site.

You can keep your site under control remotely, as the unit in able to communicate remotely using GSM data networks, via Long Range Radio, or by sending TXT messages up to 10 mobile numbers.

Supervisors are able to set up shift and patrol parameters. The GMU also boasts of an on-board remote control receiver for guard panic and assistance requests.

The TX Cellular Plus

State of the art GSM/GPRS alarm transmitter that features dual network SIM functionality. It is available with either SIM card carriers or the latest chip SIMs.

  • Option of Single or Dual SIM card operation for dual network reporting redundancy
  • GPRS reporting to a control room
  • Option of back-up SMS (to control room) if GPRS is not available
  • SMS directly to cell phones (up to 10 cell numbers)
  • SMS reporting to cell phones includes zone and partition identification in plain text
  • Can be configured as a simple alarm control panel
